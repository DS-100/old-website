Principles & Techniques of Data Science

Summary

Combining data, computation, and inferential thinking, data science is redefining how people and organizations solve challenging problems and understand the world. This intermediate level class bridges between Data 8 and upper division computer science and statistics courses as well as methods courses in other fields. In this class, students will master the data science life-cycle and learn many of the basic principles and techniques of data science spanning algorithms, statistics, machine learning, visualization, and data systems. Skills and expertise developed in this class will enable students to pursue careers in data science or apply data-science to research.
